Services

What I do

01

Film & Documentary Scoring

Original scores built from your story's DNA. Character motifs, emotional arcs, and sound design that lives inside the edit — not on top of it.

02

Brand & Commercial Audio

Sonic identities, ad scores, and ambient soundscapes for brands that want to sound like themselves, not a stock library.

03

Music Licensing

A growing catalog of original compositions available for sync. Instrumental, ambient, and piano-driven work ready for placement.

Process

How it works

01

Immerse

I watch your cuts. I read your scripts. I absorb the world you're building before I touch a single instrument.

02

Direction

I build a music lookbook — references, palettes, and a compositional direction that maps to your story's arc.

03

Compose

Themes, sketches, and demos. We iterate until every cue feels inevitable — like it grew out of the footage itself.

04

Deliver

Polished stems, masters, and any format you need. Clean handoff, no loose ends.
