About

Music built around your characters, not a template

Corporate Vibes is the scoring and music production practice of Aaron Joseph — a composer, producer, and storyteller based in Seattle. Every project starts with listening: to your story, your characters, and the emotional arc you're building toward. The result is music that feels like it was always meant to be there.

Process

How we work together

01

Listen

We talk about your project, your vision, and what the music needs to make people feel. I watch your cuts. I read your scripts.

02

Direction

I build a music lookbook — references, palettes, and a compositional direction that maps to your story's arc.

03

Compose

Themes, sketches, and demos. We iterate until every cue feels inevitable — like it grew out of the footage itself.

04

Deliver

Polished stems, masters, and any format you need. Clean handoff, no loose ends.
